PHASE 1

Awareness: Building Brand Salience & Reach

Set the foundation for your campaign by defining your brand, audience, and core media objectives.

Brand & Campaign Foundation

Define your brand's core mission, campaign goals, duration, and budget. This forms the bedrock of your strategy.

Audience Strategy

Identify your primary audience and explore expansion opportunities. Understand their media habits and passion points.

Media & Measurement

Set R&F goals, choose the optimal format mix (e.g., VRC + Non-skip), and define KPIs like Ad Recall and Brand Awareness Lift.

PHASE 2

Consideration: Engaging Audiences & Fostering Interest

Build a deeper connection with your audience by creating engaging content and leveraging creator partnerships.

Audience Engagement Strategy

Tap into the audience's "flow state" with engaging content. Leverage creator collaborations to build trust and authenticity.

Creative & Measurement

Add an "Engagement Module" (VVC + Partnership Ads). Adapt creative to be more interactive and track KPIs like VTR and Consideration Lift.

PHASE 3

Action: Driving Conversions & Sales

Turn interest into tangible business outcomes with performance-focused strategies and clear measurement.

Shopper Journey Strategy

Map the purchase path, craft compelling CTAs, and ensure your landing page is optimized for a seamless conversion experience.

Media & Measurement

Use the "Online Sales Module" (Demand Gen) and lookalike segments. Measure success with KPIs like Conversions, CPA, and ROAS.
